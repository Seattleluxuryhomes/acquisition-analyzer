## PART 2 — MARKETING KNOWLEDGE BASE (ground truth)

### The product, plainly
A mobile-first web app (installable PWA). The contractor taps a mic and describes a
job; AI turns it into a structured, **priced** bid, **translated into the client's
language**. The contractor privately sets real prices (AI prices are starting
points). The client gets a clean proposal they can read, **e-sign**, and **pay a
deposit on** by card — money goes straight to the contractor. Paid bids can sync to
QuickBooks. It also reads a contractor's price list (paste/CSV/photo) so bids use
**their** real items and prices, and has 24 **trade-specific estimators** (windows,
roofing, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, tile, concrete, etc.) that do a real takeoff.

### The core value (what to sell)
- **Get your evenings back** — no more writing bids after hours; talk it, send it.
- **Win more jobs** — same-day, professional bids beat the contractor who takes a week.
- **One bid, two languages** — talk in your language; the client reads theirs. No translating by hand.
- **Priced instantly** — your margin's built in; a real number the moment you stop talking.
- **Signed on the spot, paid by card now** — the deposit hits your account when they sign. Start the job paid.
- **Books update themselves** — signed bids sync to QuickBooks. No double entry.
- **Looks pro** — a one-truck operator suddenly looks like a real company.

### Target audience
- **Primary:** small residential contractors / remodelers, especially **bilingual
  (English/Spanish)** operators and crews. One-to-few trucks. Hate paperwork and
  recurring bills. Live on their phones. Trust other contractors over ads.
- **Secondary (vision, see "Don't oversell"):** their **subs/crews** — the
  Spanish-speaking trades a GC dispatches to.

### Positioning & messaging
- **Primary headline:** *Talk the job. Estimate in their language. Deposit today.*
- **Subhead in use:** *Like ChatGPT for contractors — say the job out loud and get a
  priced, professional estimate in your client's language. They sign and pay the
  deposit right on your phone.*
- **Proof framing:** "I used to spend my whole evening writing bids. Now I send them
  before I leave the driveway — and they're signed by dinner." (testimonial slot —
  we need a real one).
- **Reassurance line:** Free to start · No credit card · First bid in 3 minutes.

### Brand voice
Contractor-plain. Confident, concrete, a little blue-collar. Short sentences. Talk
about *time, money, and looking pro* — not "features" or "AI-powered solutions."
Avoid: revolutionary, seamless, cutting-edge, synergy. Spanish copy should be
natural contractor Spanish (not literal translation) — we have a native-feel
Spanish landing page as the reference for tone.

### Differentiation / competitors
- **Named competitor:** **Guild Assist** (AI assistant for contractors).
- **Category:** Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan (big field-service suites),
  Joist/JobFLEX (mobile estimate apps).
- **Where we win:** voice-first (talk, don't type); **bilingual by default**;
  **deposit-on-the-spot** with money direct to the contractor; dramatically cheaper;
  radically simple (productive in minutes); built for the one-truck/bilingual guy
  nobody else serves.
- **Where we're behind:** breadth (no deep scheduling/CRM/dispatch suite), no native
  app yet (PWA), zero brand/reviews. **Strategy: don't out-feature the suites —
  own the wedge** (voice + bilingual + instant deposit) for the underserved
  bilingual/one-truck contractor.

### Pricing (approved messaging)
- **Free to start, no credit card.** Free trial, then **$49/month**.
- Building/sending bids by hand is always free; AI + payments + QuickBooks are the
  paid layer. (So "it's a free app to try" is true.)
- (Internal, NOT yet built — do not advertise: a referral credit that lowers the
  monthly toward $0 as a contractor brings paying crew on, and a "founder rate-lock"
  for early adopters. Ask Ben before any "refer & save" campaign.)

### Channels to develop (in rough priority)
1. **Language-targeted paid social (FB/IG):** run **two funnels** — an English ad
   set → `/landing.html`, a Spanish ad set → `/landing-es.html`. This is the main
   reason the Spanish page exists. Tight audience: residential contractors/
   remodelers by metro + language.
2. **Word-of-mouth / crew referral:** contractors trust contractors; the supply
   house and the jobsite are the channel. (The mechanics — invite links, free crew
   seats — are DESIGNED not built; for now this is messaging + the WhatsApp share.)
3. **Concierge / direct outreach:** Ben personally onboarding the first contractors
   (sets up their account, builds them a sample bid, sends a link). A 5-part email
   follow-up sequence already exists (ask Ben for it) — marketing can refine it.
4. **WhatsApp** as a sharing + contact channel for this audience.
5. **App store listings** (coming) — needs listing copy + screenshots.
6. **Content/SEO long-tail** (later) — "voice bid app for contractors," "bilingual
   contractor estimate," trade-specific ("roofing estimate app"), Spanish-language
   queries.

### The bigger vision (context — mark clearly as vision, not shipped)
Beyond a bid tool, the direction is a **coordination network for the trades** ("the
Facebook of building"): a GC captures a job once and dispatches scope to his crew/
subs in their language; subs join free and pay when they want to bid their own work;
the network spreads along real working relationships; money flows through the
platform. **This is the north star, not a current feature** — do not put it in
acquisition copy as if it's live. It's useful for vision/brand/investor narrative.

### Don't-oversell list (accuracy guardrails)
- The crew/subs network, referral rewards, and in-app sales pipeline are **DESIGNED,
  not built.** Don't feature them as current capabilities.
- The e-signature is a lightweight approval record; consent/terms text is
  **placeholder pending legal review.** No "legally binding," no legal guarantees.
- AI prices are **starting points the contractor sets** — frame as "drafts your bid,"
  not "prices the job for you" perfectly.
- We have **no testimonials yet** — every quote in current assets is a placeholder
  and must be replaced with a real one before claiming it.
